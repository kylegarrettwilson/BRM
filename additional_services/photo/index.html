What to include in your email request

In order to help BRM fully understand the scale and scope of your BRM Website Maintenance needs, please make sure to include the following information in your email request:

  • Business Name
  • Website URL
  • Contact Information - Name(s) of contact person(s) at your company, phone number(s), and email address(es)
  • Company Location
  • A detailed and concise description of the maintenance you need completed on your website (add/editing content or photos, adding/removing features, etc.)
  • Ideal timeline for the maintenance completion

Once we have received and reviewed your request, BRM will contact you with a price quote for the maintenance requested and will provide an anticipated project completion timeline for your review.
