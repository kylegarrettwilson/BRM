What is hosting and how does it work?

Subscribe to our hassle-free hosting plan and enjoy having your website on it’s own high speed server for a low monthly payment. Once your website is complete, BRM will upload the content to your website’s server and your website will be live in a matter of minutes. Explore the buttons below to learn more about the benefits of hosting your website through BRM.

Dedicated Servers

Unlike using a shared server service for hosting your website, BRM provides you with the opportunity to host your website on its own server. This will help to increase the browser loading speed for your user, and it gives your website more storage capacity too!

Solid State Drive (SSD) Performance

We use servers that are equipped with solid state drives, which greatly increases the browsing speed for your website.

Lightening-Fast Networks

Each hypervisor has a fault tolerant and redundant 40 GPS network to ensure uptime and throughput. In simple terms, the servers are configured in such a way that the chances of having your website be down for any period of time is minimized.

Monitoring

We keep a close eye on your server’s bandwidth, disk, and CPU levels to optimize your website’s performance.
